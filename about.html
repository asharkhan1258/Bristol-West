About Us

Bristol West®: The Beginning

It all started in Florida in 1973. Bristol West began as a private passenger automobile insurance carrier for Florida residents and kept expanding its reach to more customers in more states. Bristol West Personal Auto insurance policies are now available to customers through agents and brokers in 43 states across the country.

Member of the Farmers Insurance Group of Companies®

Bristol West is a member of the Farmers Insurance Group of Companies, one of the largest property and casualty insurer groups in the United States. Farmers Insurance proudly serves more than 10 million households with over 19 million individual policies nationally.

Auto Insurance with You in Mind

Whether you are a customer seeking auto insurance, or you are an agent or broker looking for a solid insurance carrier for your customers, we’re operating with you in mind.

At Bristol West, we focus on delivering high-quality and responsive service to customers through product innovation and a broad array of systems to support our customers, agents and brokers.

For customers, we offer automobile insurance coverages at competitive prices with responsive customer service and claims professionals. We also offer flexible billing, paperless billing, a mobile app, text alert bill reminders, eSignature, online claim filing and claim tracking.

We know today’s world demands many options to pay your insurance premium, file a claim or manage your coverage — online, on your mobile device, on the phone, or in person with your agent or broker. We’re here to serve you. Easy as that.

For agents and brokers, Bristol West’s ongoing investment in technology provides leading-edge tools to manage business, improve efficiency and deliver service with ease at the point of sale.
