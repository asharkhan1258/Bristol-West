Auto Insurance 101

Bristol West® offers quality auto insurance at affordable rates for a wide range of drivers. Whether you have a less-than-perfect driving record, no prior insurance or traffic violations — even a DUI — our coverage options can help you get the coverage you want. We make getting covered as easy as possible.

You can be confident knowing Bristol West has coverage for you

Liability insurance

Helps cover the cost of the other driver's property and bodily injury expenses if you're found at fault in an accident.

Comprehensive insurance

Provides coverage for physical damage from events that are out of your control, like theft or vandalism and weather events.

Collision insurance

Helps cover damage when your vehicle collides with another car or object.

More coverage options:

Uninsured/Underinsured Motorists

This type of coverage helps protect you if someone else is responsible for an accident you’re in and the other person has no insurance or minimal insurance.

Bodily Injury and Property Damage

This covers your legal responsibility for an accident where someone else is injured or there is damage to someone else’s property.

Rental Reimbursement

If you rent a car after you have a covered loss, we will put up to $50 per day back in your pocket for up to 30 days.

Medical Payments

This coverage can pay for your medical care due to a car accident up to the policy limit.

Towing and Roadside Service

If your vehicle needs towing or assistance on the road, this coverage may help with the cost.

As a Bristol West customer, you’ll enjoy:

Permissive user coverage.

By providing a driver permission to use your vehicle, they are covered under your vehicle’s insurance.

$500 trailer physical damage coverage.

Liability coverage is provided for a trailer you own that is attached to a covered vehicle.

Rental car coverage.

If you rent a car, you’ll enjoy the same coverage as your personal, Bristol West insured vehicle.

Rideshare coverage option.

Extends your personal auto policy limits and coverage if you conduct Rideshare activities, including Uber Eats.

Not all policies, discounts, coverages and features are available in all states.

Coverages are not available in all policies. Where available, coverages may be subject to additional terms, conditions, or limitations.

Common Car Insurance Needs:

No Prior Insurance? We can help:
If you never had car insurance, or you let your car insurance lapse, Bristol West can help. We accept nearly all customers with, or without, prior car insurance at competitive rates.

Why choose Bristol West?
Bristol West offers flexible, hassle-free car insurance to fit most budgets. Get an online quote today.

If you received a DUI / DWI or OWI conviction, we can help:
Bristol West offers reasonably priced coverage if you are convicted of DUI, DWI, or OWI. We can also provide an SR-22/FR-44 certificate of financial responsibility, if needed.

If you need an SR-22 / FR-44, we can help:
An SR-22 is a certificate of financial responsibility required by some states or courts. An FR-44 is similar and is used in Florida and Virginia.

We provide Rideshare coverage:
Bristol West offers Rideshare coverage as an optional endorsement for drivers using platforms like Uber® or Lyft®. Not available in all states.

Need Car Insurance, but don't own a car, we can help:
A Non-Owner Auto insurance policy may help if you don’t own a car or don’t have regular access to a vehicle. Not available in all states.
