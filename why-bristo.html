How Bristol West can help you

Bristol West® has been serving drivers just like you for over 45 years. And as a member of the Farmers Insurance Group of Companies®, we're committed to offering quality auto insurance at an affordable price.

You may have some questions when you're shopping for auto insurance — and we have answers.

Scenario #1: I've missed a few payments

My credit score isn't where I'd like it to be. Will I be able to get auto insurance?

Solution

Bristol West has helped customers with poor credit—and no credit—find the auto insurance they're looking for. If you're facing this challenge, give us a try.

Scenario #2: I've never had car insurance

I worry about finding good coverage at a reasonable price.

Solution

We can help you get the auto insurance coverage you want whether you have no prior insurance, little or no driving history or have only had minimal prior coverage. Discounts may also be available for automatic payments, paperless billing, safe driving and multiple policies.

Scenario #3: I have a DUI on my record

Do I have auto insurance options?

Solution

Bristol West may be able to help if you've had multiple traffic violations, accidents or even a DUI. We offer coverage options for a variety of challenging situations.

As a Bristol West customer, you’ll enjoy:

Access to 24/7 policy information at BristolWest.com and our mobile app

Looking for easy access to policy information? Our website and mobile app give you convenient, secure access to policy documents, ID cards, bill payments and more.

Quality claims service

If you need to file a claim, our team of representatives will help you get back on the road. You can easily file and track your claim’s progress 24/7 at BristolWest.com.

Flexible payment options

We make paying your insurance premium easy with low down payments and convenient monthly payment plans. We accept debit cards, credit cards and checks as forms of payment.
